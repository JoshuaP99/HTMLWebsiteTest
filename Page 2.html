About Me

Hello! My name is Joshua and I've been working in the architecture world since 2020 working as an intern architect/draftsman. I also have experience in the IT world as well which I found to be one of the most rewarding experiences I've had in my professional career.

I am a very flexible worker who loves to work hands on and delve into my work. Problem solving is one of my strong suits and I always find myself to be self motivated to get the answers I need when it comes to work. Working in architecture has taught me a lot about discipline and being a diligent worker. The work can be tough and there isn't always a right answer but you have to think on your feet and always be ready to take action.

In my personal time I love to read, play games, and tinker with electronics. I love to learn how things are put together and how they work. Working with hardware and software has been a passion of mine since I was in high school and have always found myself working with electronics. My family always described me as the IT guy since I would always help everyone whenever they needed help troubleshooting. I find there is no better feeling than solving a problem or getting to the root of an issue on your own and being able to share that knowledge with others.
